KIMURA BUSINESS CONNECT

Overview

An exclusive four-day Business Connect retreat where strategic relationships are built through shared experience, martial arts mindset and performance.

Connect with high-level entrepreneurs in a premium, inspiring environment in Morocco, while training alongside elite BJJ and Kickboxing athletes.

Your Experience

THE VENUE : Hyatt Place Taghazout Bay

Business Connect will be hosted at the stunning Hyatt Place Taghazout Bay, a premium resort overlooking the Atlantic Ocean, just 40 minutes from Agadir International Airport.

Set on a cliff with panoramic views and direct beach access a few minutes away, the venue provides a calm and refined environment where all training sessions, conferences, workshops and networking dinners take place on site.

Modern rooms with private terraces, open spaces, outdoor pools, fitness and recovery facilities create the ideal setting to focus, connect and build meaningful business relationships in an exceptional Moroccan atmosphere.

TRAINING SESSION

Train each day in small groups alongside world-class athletes Cyril Benzaquen in Kickboxing and Jose Steve in Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu.

You’re free to join one discipline or both, depending on your experience, interests and energy.

Beyond the physical practice, these sessions are about sharing time, exchanging perspectives and understanding how elite athletes think, prepare and perform. It’s an opportunity to experience their discipline and mindset up close, and reflect on how it applies far beyond the mat.

BREATHWORK & MINDSET

Led by Samuel Coulon, these two workshops focus on helping you better manage your body and mind to perform with more clarity and control.

Through practical breathing techniques and mindset exercises, you’ll work on focus, emotional regulation and decision-making under pressure.

The goal is simple: leave with tools you can realistically apply in demanding professional situations.

BUSINESS & NETWORK

Throughout the retreat, you’ll connect with like-minded entrepreneurs through curated networking moments, focused business sessions and shared dinners designed for real conversations.

The program also includes:
  • A business conference led by Cyril Benzaquen
  • Exclusive exchanges with local entrepreneurs and key economic players, offering insight into business opportunities in Morocco and its growing role on the African continent

Experience the Retreat

FAQ

What is included in the Business Connect experience, and how does pricing work?

Your Business Connect experience includes all the core elements designed to foster meaningful connections, performance and personal growth:

  • A business conference led by Cyril Benzaquen
  • Mindset & Breathwork workshops led by Samuel Coulon
  • Curated business & networking sessions with fellow participants
  • Networking dinners designed to encourage meaningful conversations
  • Exclusive meetings with local entrepreneurs and key economic players, offering insights into business opportunities in Morocco
  • Daily Kickboxing training sessions with Cyril Benzaquen
  • Daily Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu training sessions with Jose Steve

Participants also benefit from preferential individual room rates (including breakfast) at the Hyatt Place Taghazout Bay, negotiated exclusively for Business Connect participants.

Pricing details depend on accommodation preferences and selected options are shared during the application process.

Do I have to participate in both Kickboxing and BJJ sessions?

Participants are free to join Kickboxing, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, or both, depending on their interests, experience and energy.

All sessions are designed to be accessible and adaptable, with a focus on mindset, discipline and shared experience rather than performance.

Do I need prior martial arts experience to attend?

No prior experience is required.

Sessions are adapted to different levels, and the emphasis is on learning, awareness and connection, not physical intensity or competition.

Is this retreat focused on sport or business?

Business Connect is a business-first experience.

Martial arts training is used as a tool to explore leadership, discipline, focus and resilience, while business sessions, conferences and networking remain at the core of the retreat.

Are flights and travel insurance included?

No. Participants are responsible for booking their flights to and from Agadir and purchasing travel insurance. We strongly recommend comprehensive travel insurance covering medical care, trip cancellation and emergencies.

What accommodation options are available?

Business Connect participants benefit from preferential accommodation rates at the Hyatt Place Taghazout Bay, including breakfast. Rooms are individual, modern and comfortable. Additional nights before or after the retreat can be requested, subject to availability.

How do I get to Taghazout?

The nearest airport is Agadir Al-Massira Airport. Airport transfers are included.

What languages are spoken during the retreat?

All sessions and workshops are conducted by speakers who are fluent in both English and French. It is expected that attendees are comfortable communicating in English, which will be the primary language of the retreat.

Am I responsible for being physically fit to participate?

Yes. Participants are responsible for ensuring they are in suitable physical condition for the activities they choose to take part in during the retreat.

Is Wi-Fi available at the venue?

Yes. Complimentary high-speed Wi-Fi is available throughout the Hyatt Place Taghazout Bay.

How do I secure my spot?

Participation is subject to application and validation. To express your interest, you can complete our application questionnaire, contact us via Instagram DM at @kimura.camp, email us at contact@kimura.camp, or book a call via our Calendly.

Can I extend my stay before or after the retreat?

Yes. Participants wishing to arrive earlier or stay longer at the Hyatt Place Taghazout Bay can request additional nights. Our team will assist with availability and preferential rates.
