## TITRE:

### BUSINESS CONNECT #1 Where Business Meets the Martial Arts Mindset

# SOUS-TITRE:

June 29 - July 2 ; 4 days connecting inspiring entrepreneurs, leaders and investors, united by a shared passion for martial arts, in an exceptional setting in Taghazout, Morocco.

# OVERVIEW:

**An exclusive four-day Business Connect retreat where strategic relationships are built through shared experience, martial arts mindset and performance.**

Connect with high-level entrepreneurs in a premium, inspiring environment in Morocco, while training alongside elite BJJ and Kickboxing athletes.

# YOUR EXPERIENCE

## THE VENUE :

**Business Connect will take place at the stunning Hyatt Place Taghazout Bay, an exceptional oceanfront venue designed for both performance and connection.**

All **training sessions, conferences, workshops, dinners and networking moments** will happen on-site, creating a seamless and immersive experience where conversations flow naturally — from structured sessions to informal exchanges.

With its refined atmosphere, open spaces and breathtaking views over the Atlantic Ocean, the venue offers the perfect balance between **focus, comfort and inspiration**, making it an ideal setting to build meaningful business relationships.

## TRAINING SESSION:

Train daily in **small groups** alongside world-class athletes **Cyril Benzaquen** (Kickboxing) and **Jose Steve** (Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu).

Each participant is free to join **one discipline or both**, depending on their interests and energy.

These sessions go beyond physical practice. Through shared training, discussions and time spent together, the athletes openly share their **mindset, discipline and approach to high-level performance** — on the mat and beyond.

Training becomes a natural catalyst for **connection, exchange and conversation**, strengthening bonds between participants around a shared passion for martial arts.

## BREATHWORK & MINDSET:

Led by **Samuel Coulon**, these two immersive workshops are designed to help participants **master their body and mind to perform at a higher level in business and life**.

Through practical, science-based breathing techniques and mindset exercises, you will improve **focus, emotional control, decision-making and recovery**.

These sessions create a powerful bridge between physical awareness and mental clarity — offering tools you can apply immediately in high-pressure professional environments.

## BUSINESS & NETWORK:

Through **curated networking moments, dedicated business sessions and shared dinners**, you’ll connect naturally with like-minded participants in an environment that encourages open conversations and long-term relationships.

The program also includes a **business conference led by Cyril Benzaquen**, as well as exclusive exchanges with **local entrepreneurs and key economic players**, offering insights into **business opportunities in Morocco** and the country’s growing role as a strategic hub on the African continent.

# OUR GUESTS

## **Cyril Benzaquen**

Multiple-time Kickboxing World Champion, entrepreneur and international speaker, Cyril Benzaquen embodies the bridge between elite performance and business leadership.

Through years of high-level competition, he has developed a mindset built on discipline, resilience and strategic execution — principles he now applies both in sport and entrepreneurship.

At Business Connect, Cyril shares not only his expertise on performance and mental strength, but also his insights on leadership, positioning and building a personal brand at the highest level.

## **Jose Steve**

Jose Steve is a high-level Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu competitor known for his technical precision and strategic approach to the art.

His journey in competitive grappling reflects the core values of Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu: adaptability, humility and long-term consistency.

During Business Connect, Jose offers participants a hands-on experience of the BJJ mindset — demonstrating how patience, timing and leverage translate directly into smarter decision-making and sustainable growth in business.

- A **business conference** led by Cyril Benzaquen
- **Mindset & Breathwork workshops** led by **Samuel Coulon**

### **Do I have to participate in both Kickboxing and BJJ sessions?**

Participants are free to join **Kickboxing, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, or both**, depending on their interests, experience and energy.

All sessions are designed to be **accessible and adaptable**, with a focus on mindset, discipline and shared experience rather than performance.

---

### **Do I need prior martial arts experience to attend?**

No prior experience is required.

Sessions are adapted to different levels, and the emphasis is on **learning, awareness and connection**, not physical intensity or competition.

---

### **Is this retreat focused on sport or business?**

Business Connect is a **business-first experience**.

Martial arts training is used as a **tool to explore leadership, discipline, focus and resilience**, while business sessions, conferences and networking remain at the core of the retreat.

---

### **Are flights and travel insurance included?**

No.

- Booking their **flights to and from Agadir**
- Purchasing **travel insurance**

---

### **What accommodation options are available?**

Business Connect participants benefit from **preferential accommodation rates** at the Hyatt Place Taghazout Bay, including breakfast.
